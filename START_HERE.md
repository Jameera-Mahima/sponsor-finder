## EXPECTED QUESTIONS FROM LEADERSHIP

### Q: When will we see money?
**A:** 3-4 months minimum. First decisions typically arrive in months 2-3. Funding follows in months 3-6.

### Q: How much can we expect?
**A:** Conservatively $350K-$500K in Year 1. Optimistically $1M+ with government award.

### Q: Which foundation is most likely to fund us?
**A:** Laurie M. Tisch Illumination Fund (perfect arts + mental health match) or New York Community Trust (largest funder).

### Q: What if we don't get funded?
**A:** Multiple strategies: reapply next cycle, pursue Tier 2 more aggressively, develop corporate sponsorship, government contracts, individual donors.

### Q: How much work is this?
**A:** 60-80 hours first 3 months for professional grant writing. Then ongoing relationship management.

### Q: Do we need a grant writer?
**A:** Strongly recommended. Professional applications have 2-3x higher success rate.
