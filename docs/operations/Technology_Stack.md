# Technology Stack

This document outlines all tools and platforms used in the fundraising campaign workflow.

## Campaign Management

### GiveLively.com
- Campaign description management
- Online donation processing
- Fundraising goal tracking and progress monitoring
- Donor registration and information collection
- Allows donors to contribute online without attending events

## Event Management

### Luma
- Event details and announcement pages
- Participant registration system
- Attendance tracking during events
- Post-event data on attendees vs. registrants
- Supports follow-up communication workflows

### Eventbrite
- Alternative event management platform
- Additional option for event registration and ticketing

## Contact & Email Management

### Salesforce
- Central contact database for all sponsors and donors
- Email campaign execution and management
- Click and open rate tracking
- Response monitoring and engagement metrics
- Report generation for marketing team
- Meeting and follow-up coordination

### Mailchimp
- Access to historical donor data
- Past participant and sponsor contact lists
- Integration with campaign email workflows
- Database of contacts from previous campaigns

## Data Sources

### Historical Databases
- Past donor information
- Previous event participant records
- Contact lists from similar campaigns
- Donation history and patterns
