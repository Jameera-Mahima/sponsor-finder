# Campaign Scenarios

This document outlines specific fundraising campaign examples for Healing NY and similar community-focused initiatives.

## Christmas Education Campaign

**Objective:** Support education for students in lower-income communities

**Details:**
- **Fundraising Goal:** $500,000
- **Target Location:** New York
- **Cause:** Help students in lower communities with their education
- **Target Sponsors:** Organizations and individuals willing to donate to educational causes
- **Campaign Type:** Direct fundraising appeal
- **Timeline:** Christmas season

## Arts Exhibition Fundraiser

**Objective:** Support disabled children in the New York area

**Details:**
- **Event Type:** Arts exhibition
- **Target Location:** Near New York
- **Beneficiaries:** Children with disabilities
- **Revenue Streams:**
  - Event attendance fees
  - Art sales from exhibition
  - Direct donations from attendees
- **Target Participants:**
  - Corporations willing to sponsor and attend
  - Individual donors and art collectors
  - Community supporters
- **Format:** In-person fundraising event combining art showcase with charitable giving
