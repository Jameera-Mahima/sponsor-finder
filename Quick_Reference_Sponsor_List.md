- Large: $150K-$500K
- Major: $500K+

### Corporate Sponsors
- Small: $10K-$25K

### Government Grants

- Large: $500K-$1M+
- Multi-year: 2-3 years

---

## RED FLAG WARNINGS

Watch out for:
- Foundations requiring "matching funds" you don't have
- RFPs with unrealistic timelines (less than 30 days)
- Funders focusing only on research (not direct services)
- Proposals requiring extensive program evaluation you can't support
- Geographic restrictions that exclude NYC

---

## SUCCESS STORY COMPARISONS

Look for grantees similar to Healing NY:
- Organizations serving mental health + education
- Community-based arts programs
- Programs serving underserved populations
- Multi-sector partnerships

**Where to Find Them:**
- Foundation annual reports (list of grantees)
- 990 tax forms (foundation grant history)
- Foundation websites (featured grants)

## FOLLOW-UP STRATEGY

After submission:
- Week 1: Confirmation of receipt
- Month 1: Check on review timeline
- Month 2-3: Updates on decision timeline
- Month 3: Polite inquiry on status
- Decision: Thank you letter either way
- Rejection: Request feedback + plan reapplication
- Award: Begin reporting relationship

---

## CONTINGENCY PLANNING

If no major awards in Year 1:
1. Reapply to Tier 1 (many multi-year cycles)
2. Pursue Tier 2 foundations more aggressively
3. Develop corporate sponsorship tier
4. Apply for government contracts
5. Seek individual donor campaigns
6. Partner with NAMI-NYC for collaborative funding
