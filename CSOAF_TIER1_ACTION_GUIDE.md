## KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

### Critical Elements for Success

1. **Clear Disability Focus**
   - Document accessibility features
   - Highlight disability services provided
   - Connect to disability justice principles
   - Include voices of disabled students/families

2. **NYC Community Connection**
   - Demonstrate local impact and relationships
   - Show involvement in NYC arts ecosystem
   - Document community partnerships
   - Emphasize geographic focus (where relevant)

3. **Strong Outcomes Data**
   - Student engagement metrics
   - Educational outcomes
   - Accessibility achievements
   - Community feedback/testimonials

4. **Professional Application Materials**
   - Well-written, compelling narratives
   - Clear, organized budgets
   - Polished presentation
   - Attention to funder guidelines

5. **Relationship Building**
   - Early outreach to program officers
   - Attendance at funder events
   - Networking in NYC arts/disability communities
   - Strategic positioning with referrals

### Common Application Weaknesses to Avoid

- Generic narratives not tailored to funder priorities
- Unclear connection between CSOAF programs and disability services
- Weak outcomes data or evaluation plans
- Overly ambitious budgets without clear justification
- Ignoring funder-specific requirements or word limits
- Late applications or missed deadlines
