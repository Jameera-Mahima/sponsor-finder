## GRANT WRITING CHECKLIST

### DOCUMENTS NEEDED FOR MOST APPLICATIONS
- [ ] 501(c)(3) tax exemption letter
- [ ] 2-year audited financial statements
- [ ] Board list with affiliations
- [ ] Program description (2-3 pages)
- [ ] Program budget (3-year projection)
- [ ] Staff bios and qualifications
- [ ] Organizational chart
- [ ] 3-5 community support letters
- [ ] Program outcomes/evaluation framework
- [ ] Executive director CV

### APPLICATION APPROACH BY FOUNDATION TYPE

**For Mental Health Funders (van Ameringen, NYHealth, etc.):**
- Emphasize trauma-informed practices
- Highlight serving underserved communities
- Include measurable mental health outcomes
- Address community mental health crisis

**For Arts Education Funders (Illumination Fund, SNF, etc.):**
- Connect arts to healing and social-emotional learning
- Show community engagement and access
- Demonstrate arts as mental health/wellness tool
- Include diverse artistic disciplines

**For Education Funders (Robin Hood, Tiger, etc.):**
- Show impact on student outcomes
- Emphasize social-emotional learning
- Include mental health component
- Demonstrate community need

**For Behavioral Health Funders (Helmsley, Elevance, etc.):**
- Focus on mental health + substance use if applicable
- Show access barriers addressed
- Prevention and treatment emphasis
- Patient-centered approach
