5. **Strategic Timing:** Submit to rolling/open opportunities first

### Important

6. Disability services clearly documented
7. NYC community connection evident
8. Board/leadership support and engagement
9. Realistic timeline and budget
10. Evaluation/outcomes plan in place

### Nice to Have

## ESCALATION POINTS

### Escalate to Leadership If:

- No responses from major funders within 2 weeks of outreach
- Consistent feedback that CSOAF is "not the right fit"
- Required documentation not available (e.g., audit, 501(c)(3))
- Organizational budget constraints prevent program implementation
- Staff capacity insufficient for applications
- Technical barriers to grant portals

### Escalate if Uncertain:
- Funder requirements seem unclear
- Application is significantly over/under word limits
- Budget doesn't align with program costs
- Program-funder fit is questionable
