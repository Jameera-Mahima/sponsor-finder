## KEY MESSAGING FOR APPLICATIONS

### Core Value Proposition for Healing NY
"Healing NY combines evidence-based mental health support with creative arts education, recognizing that arts are a powerful therapeutic tool for community healing. Our integrated approach addresses mental health gaps in underserved NYC communities while building artistic capacity and cultural resilience."

### Key Talking Points
1. **Arts as Therapy:** Visual arts, music, movement, drama as mental health intervention
2. **Community Healing:** Addressing collective trauma and building social resilience
3. **Educational Access:** Arts education as mental health support, not additional cost
4. **Underserved Populations:** Focus on communities with limited mental health resources
5. **Measurable Outcomes:** Mental health metrics + arts education achievement

### Grant Application Structure
1. Executive Summary (1 page): Mission + impact
2. Problem Statement: Mental health crisis in NYC, need for integrated solutions
3. Program Description: Arts + mental health model
4. Community Impact: Who you serve, why they matter
5. Outcomes & Evaluation: Mental health metrics, educational gains
6. Budget & Sustainability: Multi-year plan with diversified funding
7. Organizational Capacity: Team expertise, partnerships, track record

---

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID IN APPLICATIONS

- Vague mental health outcomes
- No clear community partnerships
- Unrealistic budgets
- Lack of program evaluation plan
- No evidence of arts integration benefit
- Missing letters of support from community
- Insufficient organizational capacity documentation

## SUCCESS RATES & REALISTIC EXPECTATIONS

**Average Foundation Grant Success Rates:**
- Major NYC foundations: 15-25% (Tier 1)
- Mid-sized foundations: 20-35% (Tier 2)
- Government grants: 10-20% (competitive)

**Recommendation:** Apply to minimum 8-10 different funders
**Timeline:** 3-6 months from first inquiry to first award
**Diversification:** Don't rely on single funder
